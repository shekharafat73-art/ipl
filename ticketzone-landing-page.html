❓ Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these tickets 100% genuine and valid for entry? +
Yes. All tickets we sell are 100% valid and will work at the stadium gate. We have verified sources and have delivered 500+ tickets successfully. Each ticket comes with a QR code that can be scanned at entry.
Do I pay before or after confirmation? +
Always AFTER confirmation. We first confirm ticket availability with you on WhatsApp. Only then will we send you the UPI details for payment. We will never ask for advance payment before confirming.
What if the match gets cancelled or I need a refund? +
If a match is cancelled by BCCI/IPL after you've paid, we process a full refund within 24-48 hours. If we cannot deliver tickets after taking payment, we refund 100% no questions asked. See our refund policy below.
How will I receive my ticket? +
Tickets are delivered as e-tickets (PDF/image with QR code) directly on WhatsApp and/or your email. You show it at the stadium gate — no printing needed.
How long does booking take? +
Typically under 2 hours from form submission to ticket delivery. Confirmation on WhatsApp usually happens within 30 minutes. Payment to ticket delivery takes about 1 hour.
Are you affiliated with BCCI or official IPL? +
No. TicketZone India is an independent ticket reseller / booking facilitation service. We are not affiliated with BCCI, IPL, or any official ticketing platform. Please read our full disclaimer below.
